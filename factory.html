About

4CADIA is a social impact foundation with a mission to support the open-source development of a decentralized platform to enable an open finance ecosystem in blockchain. 4CADIA foundation is non-profit and is maintained by supporting companies that receive tokenized voting rights to govern the building of the ecosystem. Our mission is to empower individuals through a tokenized free market.

Process

  • Architecture

    First our architects research and document all requirements and constraints, use cases, personas, processes and development models.

  • Development

    With agile methodology, development is broken down into small steps for accurate estimation and customer quality assurance.

  • Delivery

    Production delivery is carefully coordinated to ensure application quality and safety standards.

  • Planning

    Development begins when engineers align what needs to be done and plan each requirement to be delivered.

  • Implementation

    Each approved feature is packaged as a new increment of the application continuously and automatically.
