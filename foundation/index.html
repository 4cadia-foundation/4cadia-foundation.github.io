About

4CADIA is a social impact foundation with a mission to support the open-source development of a decentralized platform to enable an open finance ecosystem in blockchain. 4CADIA foundation is non-profit and is maintained by supporting companies that receive tokenized voting rights to govern the building of the ecosystem. Our mission is to empower individuals through a tokenized free market.
